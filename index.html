Understanding leads to being Outstanding

Many strive to be outstanding, but few recognize that the journey begins with understanding. When you take the time to listen, to learn, and to truly grasp the essence of a challenge, you position yourself to create solutions that leave a lasting mark. Outstanding people don’t chase recognition—they earn it through their unwavering commitment to understanding and growth.

Avid learner or Active learner

Some might ask, “Are you an avid learner or an active learner?” For me, it’s it’s both. Being an avid learner means you crave knowledge; being an active learner means you make that knowledge count. I’ve learned that to truly stand out, you need both. Passion for learning keeps the journey exciting, while applying what you’ve learned transforms that passion into purpose. I find joy in discovering new ideas and concepts as an avid learner, but the real magic happens when I become an active learner, turning knowledge into meaningful action. It’s this balance that fuels my progress and keeps me striving for excellence.

Build a Career Not a GET RICH QUICK Approach

Today, I had the chance to apply for an internship at the British High Commission office in Nigeria. It came with an attractive salary and benefits, but I chose to let it pass. Reading through the job requirements, I realized I was overqualified—holding a master’s degree for a role that demanded far less. Yet, what struck me wasn’t the opportunity itself, but the clarity it brought about my goals. I’ve resolved to build a career in software development, one rooted in purpose and long-term impact. As I reflected, the words of Oyedepo echoed in my mind:
“You will not find a story of outstanding success without a story of sacrifice behind it.”
Building a career isn’t about chasing the highest paycheck; it’s about investing in skills, embracing growth, and staying true to a vision that transcends immediate gains. Then, the paychecks will inevitably roll in, as money flows in the direction of value.
